Lola Project: Perry


Lola’s demonstrate archetype in this book is rebel. She did a lot illegal things in the drug society, hate the rules like law, and those people who works for the law.
In the story, Lola’s mom let Lola to have sex with other man, this made Lola thinks herself is dirty, and doesn’t have to many status. This made Lola being crazy, and wants honer. She begins to selling drugs, it is a fast way to get money and crazy. For most society, especially in China and Singapore, thinks drug are the stuff that can make society being a mess. The reason is that when more and more people begin to use drug, they cannot control themself through the time, so more and more things cannot be finished, if these happened to soldiers, the country will in danger. Accordingly, people always think drugs will hurt the society. However, Lola selling drugs here we can know Lola is crazy and wants money or honor fast even the way is illegal.
She also hate those people who work for the law, like cope (p260), because they hindered her from making money.
She also murdered a girl, just because the girl doesn’t get to finish her question. Her gang also did illegal things.
Form these event we can know that Lola did many illegal things, to rebel the law, the rule of government, her unlucky childhood made her being like this.
